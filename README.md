# CashBase.ai

**Your financial base of operations.**

---

## The Problem

Every personal finance app gets it wrong. They obsess over categorizing every $4.50 coffee transaction, building pie charts of your spending categories, and sending you alerts like "you spent 23% more on dining this month."

Nobody asked for that.

The real problem is simple:

- **How much cash do I actually have right now?**
- **What's due in the next 2 weeks?**
- **Am I going to be short anywhere?**
- **Where should I move money to avoid fees, interest, and late charges?**

That's it. That's the whole product.

---

## The Solution

One screen. Three answers.

### 1. Cash Position
Total liquid cash across all your accounts. One number at the top.

### 2. Upcoming Outflows
Every bill, credit card payment, and subscription due in the next 30 days. No surprises.

### 3. The Move
AI tells you exactly what to do: "Move $2,400 from savings ‚Üí checking before Friday to cover your Chase bill. You'll have $1,200 buffer left for rent on the 1st."

### Supported Integrations (via Plaid)

- Brokerages (Morgan Stanley, Robinhood, etc.)
- Credit cards
- Investment accounts
